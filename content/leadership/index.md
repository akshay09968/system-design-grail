# Leadership & Strategy

Everything before this section teaches you to design systems. This section teaches the layer *around* the systems — because Staff, Principal, and CTO are not "more system design": they're a change of medium. At Staff, your primary output becomes **documents and reviews**; at Principal, **strategy** — the few choices that make a thousand future choices easy; at CTO, **the organization itself** and the translation between technology and business. The technical mastery in the other twelve sections is the entry ticket; these six pages are the craft that spends it.
