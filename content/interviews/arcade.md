# The Arcade

Play is the highest-retention study mode there is — these three games train the reflexes that interviews (and incidents) actually test: **magnitude instinct**, **failure-mode recognition**, and **latency intuition**. Ten questions per run, instant explanations, and your personal best is saved in this browser. High scores here translate directly to [estimation verdicts](../foundations/estimation.md) delivered without hesitation and [failure modes named on sight](../distributed/failure-modes.md).
